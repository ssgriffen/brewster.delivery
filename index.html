WHAT IS BREWSTER?

Brewster is a mobile and web application that enables your venues' fans to order concessions and/or merchandise from their smartphone and have those items delivered to their seat.

The Brewster platform comes complete with an easy-to-use order management application that delivery runners can use during the event to ensure completion & speedy delivery of orders.

Venues can customize their menu/offerings, track orders by delivery person, print a sales report for each event, and have access to different ways to look at the order history to reconcile issues.

VALUE ADDED PROPOSTIONS

Ⅰ. Add a premiere fan experience to your venue

Ⅱ. Increase revenue through a higher per order spend and capturing lost sales due to lack of POS or lines

Ⅲ. Add a new digital marketing channel to reach your fans
