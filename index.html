WHAT IS BREWSTER?

Brewster is a mobile-responsive web application that enables your venues' fans to order concessions and/or merchandise from their smartphone and then have those items delivered to their seat.

The Brewster platform comes complete with an easy-to-use order management application that delivery runners can use during the event to ensure completion & speedy delivery of orders.

Customize menu offerings, track orders, access order history, and print sales reports all within one application. No downloads, just sales.
